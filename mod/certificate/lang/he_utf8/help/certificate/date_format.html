Format date

Choose a date format for the date on the certificate:

%DD - Day of the month, 2 digits with leading zeros.

%dd - Day of the month without leading zeros.

%ss - English ordinal suffix for the day of the month, 2 characters.

%MS - full month name according to the current locale.

%mm - Numeric representation of a month, without leading zeros.

%MM - Numeric representation of a month, with leading zeros.

%YY - A full numeric representation of a year, 4 digits.

%yy - A two digit representation of a year.

Samples:

%DD %MS %YY = 05 Janeiro 2007

%DD/%MM/%YY = 05/01/2007

%dd/%mm/%yy = 5/1/07

%DD de %MS de %YY = 05 de Janeiro de 2007

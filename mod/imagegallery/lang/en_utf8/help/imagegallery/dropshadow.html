Drop shadow

Use drop shadow effect when creating thumbnail images. This setting does not affect already made thumbnails.

NOTICE!!! You'll need PHP version 5 or greater to make drop shadow to look great. PHP version 4 does not have imagefilter-function which makes the blur effect.

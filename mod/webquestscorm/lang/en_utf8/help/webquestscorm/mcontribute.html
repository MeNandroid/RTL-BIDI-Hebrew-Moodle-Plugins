Contribution

This element shall be used to describe those entities (i.e., people, organizations) that have affected the state of the metadata (not the SCORM Content Model Component being described) instance during its development lifecycle. It has three parts:

  • Role: defines the kind or type of contribution made by the contributor
  • Entity: identifies the entity or entities that may have contributed during the development lifecycle of the metadata instance. An entity can be an individual person, organization, etc.
  • Date: identifies the date of the contribution made by the entity.
  • Description: a textual description of the date.

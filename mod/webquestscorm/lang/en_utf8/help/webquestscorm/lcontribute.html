Contribution

Description of those entities (i.e., people, organizations) that have contributed to the state of the webquest during its lifecycle (e.g., creation, edits, reviews, publications, etc.). The Contribute element enables capturing of all those individuals or organizations involved. It has three parts:

  • Role: defines the kind or type of contribution made by the contributor
  • Entity: identifies the entity or entities that may have contributed during the development lifecycle of the webquest. An entity can be an individual person, organization, etc.
  • Date: identifies the date of the contribution made by the entity.
  • Description: a textual description of the date.

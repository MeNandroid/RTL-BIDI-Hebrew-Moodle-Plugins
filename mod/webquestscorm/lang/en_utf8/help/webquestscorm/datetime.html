Date Time

The format of the date time element shall be represented in accordance with ISO8601:2000:

YYYY[-MM][-DD[Thh[:mm[:ss[.s[TZD]]]]]]]

Where:
  • YYYY = four-digit year (>=0001)
  • MM = two-digit month (01 through 12)
  • DD = two-digit day of month (01 through 31)
  • hh = two-digit hour (00 through 23)
  • mm = two-digit minute (00 through 59)
  • ss = two-digit second (00 through 59)
  • s = one or more digits representing a decimal fraction of a second
  • TZD = time zone designator

At least the four-digit year must be present. If additional parts of the DateTime are included, the character literals -, T, : and . are part of the character lexical representation for the date time.

Example: 2002-12-12
